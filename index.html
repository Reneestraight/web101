Who is Preston?

Preston is happy, energetic, normal boy. He loves playing, jumping on the trampoline with his brothers and Toy Story. Just like any other boy he loves spending time with his family and laughs like crazy. However, Preston has an indivisible disease that is wreaking havoc inside of his body.
